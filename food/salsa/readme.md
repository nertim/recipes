# Salsa
[Personal](../../readme.md)

## Ingredients
- 6 roma tomatoes
- 1 jalapeno
- 1/2 red onion
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/4 cup roughly chopped cilantro
- 1 lime juice
- 1 tsp salt (need to adjust according to taste)
- 1/4 tsp cumin

## Steps
1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend to liquid
2. Adjust lime/salt as needed by taste
